Frequently Asked Questions

Is bathtub refinishing durable? ▼

Yes, with proper care, a professionally refinished bathtub can last for 10-15 years or even longer. We use high-quality materials designed for durability and resistance to everyday wear and tear.

How long does the refinishing process take? ▼

Most bathtub refinishing jobs are completed in a single day, typically 3-5 hours. The tub usually needs to cure for 24-48 hours before it can be used again.

Is refinishing safe and eco-friendly? ▼

Absolutely! We prioritize using low-VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) and non-toxic materials. Refinishing also significantly reduces landfill waste compared to bathtub replacement, making it an environmentally responsible choice.

How much does bathtub refinishing cost compared to replacement? ▼

Refinishing typically costs 70-80% less than replacing a bathtub, saving you thousands. This includes avoiding demolition, plumbing, and tiling expenses.
